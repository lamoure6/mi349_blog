Hello, my name is Danielle and I am a senior at Michigan State University. I am majoring in computer science with a cognate in media and information, and minoring in graphic design.

Web design has been a big interest of mine, and I could really see myself pursueing it, or something similar to it, in my future. I currently work as a graphic designer for the College of Engineering K-12 Outreach Office at MSU, and took an internship as a Product Design at Ford for this summer. Both of these experiences are really helping me to find my passion and realize how much the design industry interests me.
